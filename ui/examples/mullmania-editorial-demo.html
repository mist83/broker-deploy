Make the builder feel designed.

This is the typographic move the frontend skill wants: keep the work surface practical, then spend the visual budget on the product name, the opening composition, and a small number of memorable type moments.

2 typefaces + mono Display for theater, sans for product UI, mono for labels and diagnostics.
1 accent color Cobalt carries action and state; orange appears only as a controlled warm counterpoint.
